Detailed Introduction / é¡¹ç›®ç®€ä»‹

EN. Staff2Sound is an Optical Music Recognition (OMR) system that converts sheet music images into structured musical representations and playable results. Instead of directly generating audio end-to-end, we focus on symbol-level detection and reconstruction, which improves interpretability and robustness in dense musical layouts.

We fine-tune a YOLO-based object detector to localize musical symbols under staff-aware cropping. To recover staff geometry, we apply a filter-based staffline detection module. Finally, a rule-based pitch reconstruction step maps detected notes to pitches using geometry + music theory constraints, producing a reliable MIDI output for playback and evaluation.
